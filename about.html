The Experience

Our primary objective with couch.to.gym is to offer an easily accessible and realistic gym experience through virtual reality. Our platform has over 25 different types of gym equipment and many additional features to maximize the value of your purchase.

Additional Features

Couch.to.Gym enhances user experience with additional in-game features, such as unlockable gym areas and items like pre-workout and smelling salts. The platform incorporates a lifelike progression system, allowing users to build strength alongside gameplay and unlock heavier weights over time.

The lifelike progression system not only applies to strength level but also your in-game physical appearance. New players will start off with an untrained figure and can develop the character's body as you play.
